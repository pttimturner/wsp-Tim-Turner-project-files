Install

At Signs By Design we can install all types of signage. We install signs for both local clients and national accounts. Our installation service includes surveys, permitting and installations. Besides installation, we also service, repair, and refurbish existing signage and lights. Our experienced staff can do whatever is required for your particular job. And as always, our success depends on your satisfaction.

Repair/Refurbish

Whether your sign lights partially, bad weather tears up your sign or it's been hit, our professional staff can take care of any problem you have with your sign. We have experience with repairing and servicing pylons, cabinets, channel letters, neon, LED, digital message boards and more. Our service extends to national accounts, commercial properties, restaurant chains, and one store owners. Our experienced staff can do whatever is required for your particular job. If you're not sure if we can repair or service your sign, please call or email and we'll be glad to discuss your needs.

Surveys/Permits

At times surveys are needed to determine what type and size of signage are needed for a location. We survey signs for local clients and national accounts. For new installations and some face replacements, permits are required. Our staff has the capability of acquiring all necessary permits for your sign installation. Remember that most areas require electrical hookups to be permitted and installed by a certified electrician. Some tenants have specific signage criteria, so we strongly recommend you check with your building landlord before choosing what type of signage to install. If you're not sure if a permit is required, or if a survey is necessary, give us a call or email and we'll be glad to assist you.

Lighting

As one of Signs by Design services, we can change light bulbs both internally and externally. These bulbs can be found in parking lot lights, signs, walpacks, decorative lights and security lights. We work with single store owners as well as facility maintenance companies. We can install timers and photo cells if needed. Signs by Design can also install light fixtures and parking lot poles. Whether it's changing one bulb or bulbs for an entire facility, give us a call or email us and we'll be glad to discuss your lighting needs.
